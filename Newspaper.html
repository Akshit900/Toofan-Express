Don’t stay bail orders casually: SC to courts

New Delhi The Supreme Court on Thursday condemned the practice of higher courts casually staying bail orders granted by trial courts,
particularly when the accused is neither a terrorist nor considered a threat to society. The apex court underscored that such stays should not be granted
merely at the behest of investigating agencies, especially when detailed reasons for granting bail have been provided by the trial courts.
